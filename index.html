FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you need to know about SalesBuddy, ConnectoStore, and how they fit together.

What is SalesBuddy?

SalesBuddy is an AI-powered Facebook Messenger chatbot that connects to your Facebook Page, automatically answers customer questions, captures orders, syncs with your inventory, and hands off to a human when needed.

What is ConnectoStore?

ConnectoStore is a full-stack ecommerce platform for managing your online business end-to-end — inventory, orders, delivery tracking, supply chain, and AI analytics, all in one place.

Can I use SalesBuddy and ConnectoStore separately?

Yes. Each product works independently. Used together, they share inventory and order data in real time for a completely unified operation.

How long does setup take?

SalesBuddy goes live in about 5 minutes — connect your Facebook Page and upload your product list. ConnectoStore onboarding is typically completed within a day with guided setup and migration support.

Do you support Bangladeshi f-commerce?

Absolutely. Both products are built with Bangladeshi f-commerce in mind, including BDT currency, local courier integrations, and Messenger-first shopping behavior.
